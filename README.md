I'm a master's student at **USTC** and a member of the **Data Darkness Lab (DDL)**, advised by [**Prof. Xike Xie**](https://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~xkxie/). I am currently interested in **post-training** and **Graph4LLM**, with an emphasis on model reasoning, adaptation, structured knowledge, and efficient learning and inference.

### R-GFM

**Learning Graph Foundation Models on Riemannian Graph-of-Graphs**  
**ICML 2026**

**Haokun Liu**, Zezhong Ding, Xike Xie

R-GFM is a scale-adaptive and geometry-aware GFM for transferable representation learning across heterogeneous graph domains. It combines multi-scale modeling with mixture-of-Riemannian-experts routing and achieves state-of-the-art results with up to **49% relative improvement**.
